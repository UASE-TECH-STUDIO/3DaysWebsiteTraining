About Me

I am Abdullahi Mustapha, a passionate web development enthusiast currently being coached by UASE Tech Studio. My journey into web design began with a fascination for how websites are built and the desire to create beautiful, functional digital experiences.

I specialize in using HTML to structure content and CSS to bring designs to life. I love the challenge of transforming ideas into responsive web pages that look great on any device, from large desktops to mobile phones.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new tech trends, reading about design principles, or contributing to open-source projects. I'm always eager to learn and grow, and I believe in the power of continuous improvement in the ever-evolving world of web development.
